CEVIO Activities with Tohoku Kiritan

In Summer of 2022, I bit on an irresistable DLSite sale and purchased Tohoku Kiritan for about $90... in the dead of night, I'll get excited and start making covers. Everything heard here is mixed by an anonymous friend. My Kiritan is usually set to +0.20 maturity.

OLD Covers

Deep Sea Girl (深海少女) - yuuyu-P

My first CEVIO AI cover with base Kiritan. It took months to realize I had not updated to her 1.1.0 version. As far as a first cover goes I am pleased with it, but it's overworked. I actually reeled back on some adjustments *because* I noticed lines were unnecessarily edited.

Fukashigi no Carte (不可思議のカルテ) - Kawai Luigi, Kodama Ameko

My second completed cover. It took much more effort for Kiritan to sing this passably as she needed multiple timing, pitch, and vibrato adjustments. I took advantage of the then-new huskiness slider to convey the softness of the original vocalists.

Kiritan's vowel -> i notes are on an early VOCALOID2 level of choppy without adjustment. And she just loves to drift off key.
