Meta Knight

  • Gender: ♂
  • Height: 159cm (5'2")
  • Species: ???

Leader of Dedede's royal guard and the Meta-Knights. Once a prominent member of the Star Warriors, a famous intergalactic mercenary group, he swore off his duties after being traumatized by missions fighting Dark Matter. He's not as uptight and obedient as he seems, but is a truly capable knight.

He and Dedede are actually on good terms and talk often, but he's flaky when it comes to following his orders. He lives in the castle, so it isn't hard to get a hold of him when he's slacking.
