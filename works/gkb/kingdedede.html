King Dedede

  • Gender: ♂
  • Height: 184cm (6'2")
  • Species: Incarnation/spirit

Self-proclaimed King of Dreamland. Assumed to be childish and dramatic, but is shockingly perceptive and treats most things so casually that it's concerning. He operates on purely on vibes.

Likes festivals, attention, gimmicky things, and drinking and smoking at the castle bar. Dislikes vary.

Kirby rarely wins against his hammer. Definitely a sore loser though.

History

King Dedede is the star's reincarnated spirit, called an incarnation; he was previously killed by Nil in a rage. (Which is why he always feels entitled to the star and has better resistance to DM possession.) He was reborn an adult centuries later when the star finally recovered from Nil's damages.
