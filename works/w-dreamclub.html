Dream C Club

For people with pure hearts

A simulation ADV series active in the early 2010's, primarily on PS3 and XBOX 360. You assume the role of an average Joe that wins a year-long membership to the coveted Dream Club, where maidens pure of heart look forward to chatting and drinking with you. The main goal in most titles is to pursue a legitimate relationship with one of the hostesses by the end of the membership period. Every hostess has their reasons for working there as well as friends that the player should maintain good relationships with to become closer to the chosen love interest.

Translated materials and discussions exist but are sparse and mostly forgotten. The series has been inactive since 2014 but the hostesses have cameoed in the publisher's other games, such as Earth Defense Force 5 on Steam. As a fan of the (tropey) cast, style, and music, I wanted to gather some of that in one place and make a fun page to celebrate the series.

Short ver. Karaoke files

Files ripped from Dream C Club ZERO Special Edipyon! (PS3, 2013), Dream C Club (XBOX360, 2009), and Dream C Club Gogo. (PS3, 2014). Note that while these files have been converted to .WAV, most were not stored in CD-level quality. Real, ripped from the XBOX360 DLC, was the only exception to this for some reason, either an oversight or how the DLC was distributed when the user downloaded it.
