5/20/2024: MIKU EXPO 2024 at San Jose Civic

Through the dark of the night, I know we will be alright, there's a universe of positivity beyond the sky

Ticket experience

Dreadful. But probably the luckiest I've ever been when it's come to grabbing tickets, especially for a show this popular. I aimed for GA tickets during the Ticketmaster presale for Crunchyroll subscribers, but its waiting line opened at 7AM for me. Regardless, I woke up shortly before and struggled all morning to find tickets for either the Monday or Tuesday shows available at SJ, until, four hours later, the Tuesday show's page suddenly listed tickets at GA price ($55)! One for me and two for my confidential guests. You can tell from my desperation that I really, really wanted to go.
