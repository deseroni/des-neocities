MAD RAT DEAD Original Soundtrack

  • Release date: 10/29/2020
  • Genre: Electronic (swing, pop, house)
  • Featuring: DYES IWASAKI, Camellia, a_hisa, Yo Oyama, Kazuya Takasu, xaki, Johngarabushi, Teisena (Violin)
  • Runtime: 36 tracks, 2:28:43

The GOAT of 2020, revered for its distinctive style and rhythm/action platformer mix, albeit overpriced at MSRP. NIS got their hands on an all-star group of in-house and outside musicians for this title; westerners are probably most familiar with Camellia, who was responsible for tracks pertaining to one of the main characters. It's listenable independent of the game but obviously best experienced with the context and beatmaps provided by the latter.

My favorite track (and its accompanying stage, 6-3) is Mini MOON.
