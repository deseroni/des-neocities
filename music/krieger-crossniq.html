UPLINK - CROSSNIQ+ Original Sound

  • Release date: 10/4/2019
  • Genre: DnB, jungle, house
  • Featuring: ViRiX Dreamcore (David Mckee), DV-i (D.V. Caputo)
  • Runtime: 14 tracks (2 hidden), 33:59

Slide puzzles but awesome: this is CROSSNIQ, a stylish and addictive puzzle game, developed mainly by Max Krieger. His website and social media accounts mirror his admiration for late 90's and y2k graphic design, the excitement for unorthodoxy and technology at the turn of the century. The game's responsiveness, UI, and gameplay loop are enhanced tenfold by the soundtrack, which was described by ViRiX as Ape Escape-like (Soichi Terada's jungle). To that end there are clear influences in Super Monkey Ball (Tomita, Shoji) and Tetris: The Grand Master (Hosoe, Saso).
