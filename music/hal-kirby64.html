Hoshi no Kirby 64 Original Soundtrack

  • Release date: 4/26/2000
  • Featuring: Jun Ishikawa, Hirokazu Ando
  • Runtime: 39 tracks, 1:02:31

Let's celebrate the release of Forgotten Land with some Kirby in the library. This album isn't too exciting on its own, it's a simple remaster, but it includes two exclusive "Club Mixes." This practice is now common in modern Kirby CD releases, so, hey. Awesome soundtrack with classic Korg M1/Fairlight/etc. samples the games were known for until post-RTDL.

64 was my first Kirby game, found at a hotel room in Nevada, though I didn't replay it until over a decade later. It can be a pain in the behind like DL2 is. What are your memories with Kirby 64?
