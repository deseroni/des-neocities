i-style Project - A Sugar Business

  • Release date: 4/27/2012
  • Genre: EDM (Trance, dubstep)
  • Featuring: Taishi (Electro.muster), Wata, Grok, & kato.yoshihal (WOM), Aoki Lapis (vocals)
  • Runtime: 5 tracks, 32:46

The second album to feature Aoki Lapis, released by her parent company i-style Project. It's hard to find information on them as they are mostly inactive (save for a recent TTS software for her released under CoeAvatar) but i-style was fairly supportive of Lapis in her heyday... she just didn't catch on like they hoped she would. Regardless, she's my second favorite VOCALOID.

Did you know Lapis and EDM are a pretty good combo? A sweet, light vocal against powerful and tense tracks... it's unique.
