Eve - Otogi

  • Release date: 2/06/2019
  • Genre: J-rock
  • Featuring: Eve, Numa (arrangement)
  • Runtime: 11 tracks, 41:56

Eve is known for the fantastical and high-production music videos that accompany his songs, but he's a good vocalist and lyricist in his own right. He and his team take very simple concepts, generally about the human condition, and just run wild with them. Many of his songs, even ones featured on this album, are connected visually and lyrically. 'Last Dance' is perfect evidence of such.

'Outsider' was my first exposure to Eve, but 'Tokyo Ghetto' is my favorite track from this album.
