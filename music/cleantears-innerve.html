Clean Tears - INNERVE

  • Release date: 5/05/2010
  • Genre: Techno, trance
  • Featuring: Hatsune Miku (vocals)
  • Runtime: 9 tracks, 53:59

Released under Clean Tears' doujin circle, S.C.X.

An album I stumbled upon by an artist I had heard of from their 2018 release, Heat Haze. A high-quality selection of modern EDM. It's surprisingly ambient even with a vocal like Miku's. As is customary with this genre, every track runs for at least 6 minutes. It sports a sharp cover illustration too.
