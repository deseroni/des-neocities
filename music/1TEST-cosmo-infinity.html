cosMo/BouSouP - ∞-InfinitY-

  • Release date: 6/29/2008
  • Genre: Progressive rock, electronic
  • Featuring: Hatsune Miku (vocals)
  • Runtime: 9 tracks, 34:48

Released under cosMo's circle CHEMICAL SYSTEM.

This release is literally and conceptually a prototype of cosMo's 2009 album InfiniteHOLIC. It marks the beginning of cosMo's numerous iterations of his own tracks, as well as narratively-driven tracklist orders, so this is a good starting point for understanding his discography.

CosMo was one of many producers at the time that wrote of Hatsune Miku's self-awareness, her relationships with her users, and her potential to be a universally loved singer. His take follows Miku gaining awareness of being commercial software and her various reactions, with her Master deciding her fate. Sometimes she's uninstalled, reinstalled, and others she goes mad (positive and negative). Such a classic album with an endearing young "roughness" to cosMo's instrumentation and ideas. Has my favorite version of "0."
